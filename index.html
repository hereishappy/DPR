Help & FAQ

How to use this app?

Fill in your information, add work details, and get productivity reports.

How is productivity calculated?

Productivity = (Erection + (Dismantling/2)) / Total Manhours

Can I edit my entries?

Yes, you can go back to previous steps using the Back button.

How do I add both erection and dismantling?

After adding erection details, click "Add Dismantling" to switch and add dismantling data.
